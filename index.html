Hey there! My name is Elshaday Getahun and I am a second year Sociology major. I am interested in entering the field of public health and bettering the health care system. I look forward to fighting the issues that certain groups of individuals deal with in the system. I am an outgoing person that loves to constantly try new things. I love to go out and try new foods and different settings of places that I have never been to. I also enjoy making friends and meeting new people whenever I can. I am interested in public health and its social aspect in the sense that I want to make things better in the healthcare system for low income families who struggle with this issue. I am very passionate about fixing the issues that minorities face when trying to access health care. I also am interested in community service work and giving back to my community.

I have a fun time going to the beach and going on hikes near the mountains. For fun, I like to go bowling and writing in my journal. Overall, I am a person that loves to have with other people and create friendships with people that have the same goal as me. As a person, I enjoy giving out a free hand to people whenever I can and sticking out for my friends and family. Living life while having fun and learning new things, is one of my goals that I try to accomplish every single day.
